Sania One Day Hiring

"Workforce in 9 Hours, Not 9 Days."

Sania One Day Hiring offers rapid and reliable staff deployment for businesses needing verified workers for single-day assignments, short shifts, and project-based work.

  • Blue & Grey Collar Staffing: Warehouse workers, pickers, loaders, cashiers, helpers, and more
  • Deployment in 24–48 Hours: Or even the same day for urgent needs
  • Verified & Trained Workforce: Every candidate is pre-screened, verified, and ready

Who It's For:

  • Warehouses & Logistics
  • Retail Stores & Supermarkets
  • Events & Exhibitions
  • Restaurants & Cloud Kitchens
  • Small & Mid-sized Businesses

How It Works:

1

Requirement Raised – Tell us your need via call, form, or WhatsApp

2

Profiles Shared – Within hours, verified candidates are matched

3

Same Day Deployment – Staff reports for duty on the same day or within 24 hours

One Day Hiring Service

Sania Job Mitra

"Your Nearest Job Friend."

Sania Job Mitra is a kiosk-based model that connects job seekers with verified opportunities locally, serving as a community touchpoint for employment.

  • Walk-in Job Support: Local candidates can walk in, get verified, and apply
  • Job Seeker Community Building: We enable micro-local job discovery
  • Kiosks Across India: Compact setups in malls, markets, and residential zones

Who It's For:

  • Job seekers with limited internet access
  • Employers hiring locally
  • Daily wage workers and fresher candidates

How It Works:

1

Job Seekers Visit Mitra Kiosk – Submit their basic documents

2

Verification & Shortlisting – Local openings are matched

3

Walk-in Interviews or On-Spot Deployment – As per availability
